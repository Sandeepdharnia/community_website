Welcome to the Community Hub

Your One-Stop Place for Community Life

Welcome to the Zürich Community Center - your reliable hub for staying connected, informed, and involved. Whether you're looking for upcoming events, local news, or simply curious about what’s happening around you, you’ve come to the right place. From workshops and cultural festivals to language cafés and children's activities, we host a wide range of programs designed to bring people together and strengthen our sense of community. We also believe in giving everyone a voice — that's why our local news section is open to contributions from residents who want to share stories, updates, or announcements. This platform was proudly developed with the support of Powercoders, a tech academy empowering refugees and migrants through coding. We're thankful for their partnership and the talented developers who helped bring this website to life. Together, we're building a more connected and inclusive Zürich — one story, one event, and one click at a time.
